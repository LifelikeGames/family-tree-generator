Random Family Tree Generator 3.0

Enter (optional) information about a patriarch and/or matriarch, then click the Generate button to create their family tree. Names will be generated automatically along with a random MBTI personality type. To reproduce a random tree, save the value of Random Seed and enter it again later. To get the family as of a particular year, fill in Current Year; otherwise expand the tree with the Family buttons. Edit a name and press return to change it, or press the R button to get a new random name.
